Residential Open Living Space

A residential unit designed from scratch for a young outgoing couple, with an approach to Minimal Modern style and white, gray, and black color palette. Based on the client profile, brick, and wood surfaces make the environment warm and homey. This is a single project.

Convenience Store

This Convenience store is placed on the ground floor of a residential community for the wellness and comfort of residents. The store includes areas for food, cleaning supplies, notions, books and magazines, child’s stuff, flowers, and gift boxes. The loading deck is located at the back of the building. This is a single project.

Klotz Student Health Care Center

Klotz Student Health Care Center at the California State University, Northridge, offers health care to students in a three-story building. The design concept is to save energy through solar panels, using sustainable materials such as bamboo and concrete, and a botanical approach to create a healthy space for its users and environment.

Steelcase Next Competition 2023

This office is designed for 46 architects, designers, managers, and coordinators with an approach to Generation Z's needs in the work environment. This innovative workplace is poised to be a hub for design and creativity, fostering a vibrant environment for the next generation of forward-thinking designers. This place is a flowing flexible sustainable modern office for working, collaborating, and having a sense of home. This individual project is one of the two candidate projects to participate in the Next Office Competition 2023.

Senior Thesis Project

A Large Home Environment for Foster Children This place is an individual senior thesis project which is designed for children between 6 to 15 years old to give them a safe home, and physical, emotional, and mental support. This is a place that provides children with after-school activities such as a library, music class, basketball room, outdoor area, theater, party zone, private rooms, and family rooms. This project has an approach to trauma-informed design, color theory, light, and nature.

Residential Two-Story Guest House

Turning an existing pool house in Beverly Hills into a two-story two-bedroom guest house with a modern, warm, and neutral style. I designed an alternative design to provide to the client in the schematic design step.
